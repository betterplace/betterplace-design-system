# Design Principles

## Human Centered:

Specify context, be empathetic, test often, iterate

## Accessible:

All our product should be able to be used by as many people as possible. More on that in accessibility.

## Joyful:

Doing something good should feel good and be fun.

## Responsive:

Our products should work on all devices. Layouts should adapt and support.
